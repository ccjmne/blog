ccjmne's tech blog

One meticulous fellow yelling at clouds

I curate here a technical flight manual that publishes practical guides towards mastering your software development craft.

I also share some ramblings that are more conceptual or less grounded in relevance and practicality, but may be consumed more recreationally—or precautionarily.

I am fairly slow and deliberate.
I intend to keep putting out articles at the approximate rate of one per week, but my propensity for ranting tangents significantly hinders the production of articles getting to the point at all, let alone straight.
If you stick around a short while, I'm sure you'll come to find the irony in that.

User guide

This Web site's colour scheme automatically adjusts to your preference as indicated by your Web browser, which should forward that of your desktop environment; though it currently looks better in light mode.

Web browsers supporting View Transitions will animate, in a non-nauseating manner, various anchor points as you peruse the site. This functionality may be disabled by indicating, through your browser again, your preference for reduced motion.
